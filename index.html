MISSION STATEMENT

The Pointe Circle is a volunteer-driven non-profit organization built on our shared passion for dance, our commitment to fostering a supportive community, and our dedication to meaningful service. We welcome dancers of all genres, artists of other disciplines, and anyone eager to contribute through volunteer work. Together, we celebrate the joy of movement while making a lasting impact through performances, collaborations, and community service.

Send your Zelle transfer to
The Pointe Circle
thepointecircle@gmail.com
  1. Open Zelle in your own bank's app (no account or routing numbers needed).
  2. Enter thepointecircle@gmail.com as the recipient.
  3. Confirm your bank displays the recipient name as "The Pointe Circle" — see the warning below.
  4. Enter your amount and send. Your own bank handles the transfer; we never see your account details.
⚠️

Always verify before sending: when you enter our email in Zelle, your bank should show the registered name "The Pointe Circle". If a different name appears, do not send the money — stop and email us at thepointecircle@gmail.com to confirm before trying again. This protects you from sending funds to the wrong account.

This site never collects or stores card, bank, or account numbers. Your Zelle transfer is confirmed by your own bank, not by this website — the button above simply opens an email to let us know to expect your gift and send a thank-you and receipt.
